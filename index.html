About Coach Tiana

COACH

Hi, I'm Tiana — aka Coach Tiana. I became a swim coach because, as a child, I almost drowned. More than once.

One moment that stayed with me happened at Coney Island, my hometown. It was summer. The beach was packed. I walked a little too far out and the current pulled me even farther in. I started swallowing water. I tried to scream — no sound came out. I flailed my arms desperately trying to get someone's attention. There were adults all around me. No one noticed.

By the grace of God, the current shifted and my feet touched sand again. I walked out shaken, terrified, and carrying a deep fear of water that stayed with me for years. It wasn't until I was around 11 that my uncle learned what had happened — and made it his mission to teach me how to swim. Not just to move in the water, but to feel safe in it.

That experience changed my life. And it's why I teach parents this every chance I get: Drowning is fast. Drowning is silent. A crowded beach didn't save me that day. Skills, awareness, and preparation do.

This is why I coach. This is why survival skills come first in every single lesson. And this is why I believe every child deserves to feel confident and capable in the water.
